Welcome to your Email AI Agent!

Let's quickly walk through the steps to get you started.

1. Dashboard

This is your home screen. You'll see a high-level overview of your campaign performance, active campaigns, and AI-driven insights once you start sending emails.

2. RAG Training

This is the "brain" of your AI. Upload your documents here (like clinical data, product info, or marketing guidelines). The AI will use this knowledge to write accurate and compliant emails.

3. Campaign Builder

This is your main workspace. Here you will define your campaign's goals, select a target specialty, choose a tone, and use the AI to generate email content based on your RAG documents.

4. Generate Campaign using AI

Use this for a more free-form AI experience. Instead of selecting RAG docs, you can give the AI direct instructions (prompts) to write email copy from scratch. It's great for creative brainstorming.

5. Analytics

After you send campaigns, this is where you'll track success. Connect your email provider (like Mailchimp) to see open rates, click-through rates, and other key performance indicators.

6. View / Upload Templates

Manage your HTML email templates. You can upload your company's existing templates here. The AI will inject its generated content into the template you select in the Campaign Builder.

7. Settings

Configure your integrations here. Connect to your email service provider (like Mailjet or Mailchimp) and set up other system preferences.
